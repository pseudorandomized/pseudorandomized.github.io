Antonis Antonopoulos

Computation and Reasoning Laboratory (CoReLab), National Technical University of Athens

I'm a Theoretical Computer Science Ph.D. student, member of Computation and Reasoning Laboratory of NTUA. My area of study is Computational Complexity, mainly Pseudorandomness & Derandomization, Circuit Complexity, Fine-Grained Complexity and the relations among them. Previously, I studied Applied Mathematics in NTUA with majors in Applied Analysis and Mathematics of Computer Science.
